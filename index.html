Selection policy

What gets sent to human rereview?

The queue is generated from training metadata and labels. A sample is prioritized when it creates risk for subgroup evaluation or training quality.

01

Missing age or sex

Missing demographic metadata blocks subgroup fairness evaluation, so these cases are flagged first.

age is missing OR sex is missing
02

Underrepresented intersection

Samples from low-coverage age x sex groups are reviewed before they drive training decisions.

ratio_to_uniform < 0.8
03

Rare diagnosis within group

Rare diagnosis-by-group combinations are checked because label noise has higher impact where examples are scarce.

diagnosis_age_x_sex_n < 25
